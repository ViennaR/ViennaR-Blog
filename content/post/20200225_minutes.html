For the first 2020 meetup we gathered in late February, just days before R’s official 20 year birthday.
The great location this time was IBM Austria’s headoffice in the heart of Vienna and drinks and snacks were generously provided by the hosts.

TALK ONE (twice Thomas and Rudolf)

The first presentation was held by IBM’s own (and ViennaR meetup member) Thomas [Strehl], Thomas [Weinrich] and Rudolf [Pailer].
The talk focused on application of the CRISP-DM approach for putting ML models (CRSIPML) in R into production. The slides, which include an introduction to CRISPML via a real life business case can be found here.
You can also check out the recording of their talk

TALK TWO (Mario)

The second speaker was (Quantargo’s and ViennaR meetup Co-Organizer) Mario [Annau].
Mario introduced us to a different approach towards R in production.
Where the colleagues form IBM focused on the classic (CI/CD) build pipeline and tools, Mario was showing RStudio PRO’s solutions, which basically cover the deployment process via one push button functionality.
He finished his round with a gentle introduction to Lambda R functions (so called ‘QBits’) to deploy R functions based on serverless technologies within seconds.

Thanks again everyone for attending and IBM for being such great hosts. Hope to see you all again soon at our next Meetup in March.
